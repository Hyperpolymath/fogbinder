/* DESIGN PHILOSOPHY */

Ambiguity as Feature:
Fogbinder treats epistemic uncertainty as a feature, not a bug. We embrace contradiction, mood, and mystery as valuable dimensions of scholarly exploration.

Type-Driven Design:
Types encode philosophical commitments. The ReScript type system prevents entire classes of conceptual errors.

Offline-First:
No external API calls, no tracking, no telemetry. Your research stays private.

Accessibility:
WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Ambiguity should never mean inaccessibility.
